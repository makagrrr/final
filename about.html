About Us

Café Dorrance is a Contemporary restaurant creating unique dishes with a focus on local ingredients and inspired by classical training. The interior is rich with original architectural details and antique mirrors.

From chefs who create exciting new flavors, to crew members who know exactly how you want your drink – we prioritize what you need to get you on your way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an environment where absolute guest satisfaction is our highest priority and provide our guests with a comfortable, quality, and satisfying dining experience using local, seasonal ingredients in classic dishes paired with select wine.

We value teamwork, honesty, community involvement, dedication to the culinary arts, and transparency.
