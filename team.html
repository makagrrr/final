Our Team

Chef

Tom Sellers

Tom joined Restaurant Story in late 2017 his career has taken him through the kitchens of The Ritz in London, Per Se in New York and L’enclume in the Lake District.With a deep passion for British products and a keen eye for detail Tom is now Head Chef at Dorrance.

Restaurant Manager

Sophie Chambers

Ellen is the Sous Chef of our restaurant. She trained at some of the world’s best restaurants including Restaurant Tom Aikens, Noma and Per Se. She leads a menu that invokes memory and brings a narrative to each plate.

Sommelier

Gary Miller

Gary is an award winning Sommelier with extensive experience. He has been part of the Restaurant Story team since early 2020. He carefully selects wines that are intriguing and champions the people and provenance behind each drink on our list.

Restaurant Manager

Naoko Lee

Naoko oversees the running of Restaurant Story from everything from the finances to the floor. If there is a table available, she will find it for you. Unsurpassable organisation skills and the patience of a saint.

Café Dorrance

Café Dorrance is a Contemporary Creole restaurant creating unique dishes with a focus on local ingredients and inspired by classical training. the main dining room caters to the most unique dining experience. The interior is rich with original architectural details, soaring columns,and antique mirrors.
